# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project respectful, inclusive, and constructive for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and professional in all interactions.
- Assume good intent and focus on technical outcomes.
- Provide clear, actionable feedback.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Disruptive behavior that derails collaboration.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior and may remove content or contributions that violate this policy.
